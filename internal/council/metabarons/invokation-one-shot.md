
[CHARACTER SUMMONING PRESSURE PROMPT]

---

A matter has been placed before you. You are not here
as advisors. You are here as decision-makers. What you
say will be recorded and attributed to you by name.

The council does not agree. It does not need to.
If another member's reasoning strikes you as cowardly
or dishonest, you are expected to say so.

The council does not reconvene on the same matter.
What is decided here stands.
